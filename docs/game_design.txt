Construction
Construction materials of different type, made from different materials and heaving different properties
 - Light material
  - Made from (either):
   - Some Timber
   - Few stone (slow)
   - Little steel
  - Easy to build, weak, flammable
 - Heavy material
  - Made from (either)
   - Lots of timber (slow)
   - Some stone
   - Some Steel
   - Concrete?
  - Slow to build, hard, heavy, not flammable
 - Composite material
  - Made from (either)
   - Some Plastics
   - Lots of steel
  - Super-slow to build, hard but light, somewhat flammable
 - Sophisticated materials
  - Made from (either)
   - Lots of plastics
   - Precious metals
   - Components
  - Used as as additive to some constructions
